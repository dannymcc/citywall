CityWall sets a dark, roads-only street-map of the town or city you're in as your home
and lock-screen wallpaper, and refreshes it as you travel.

Features:
* Roads-only OpenStreetMap street-maps on a dark background — no labels, no clutter
* Several colour schemes (including a light "Daylight" theme), adjustable zoom, and a
  choice of how rivers are drawn
* Set the wallpaper on the home screen, lock screen, or both
* Map your real location, the country's capital, or any place you pick manually
* Optional "Pathfinder" leaderboard: be the first to a city and claim it
* Optionally mark a chosen country's embassies on the map

CityWall uses approximate location only and works with a self-hosted map server.
